📊 Marketplace Performance Analytics (Tableau)

🚀 Project Overview
This project delivers an end-to-end E-Commerce Marketplace Analytics dashboard built using Tableau. It transforms raw transactional data into meaningful insights to analyze revenue performance, cost efficiency, and customer demand patterns across product categories.

🎯 Objectives
- Analyze revenue distribution across categories
- Evaluate freight cost impact on sales
- Identify top-performing and underperforming segments
- Track order trends and seasonality
- Understand market share contribution by category

📌 Key Features & Dashboards
🔹 Revenue vs Freight Cost
Compares sales with logistics cost
Identifies categories with high cost but low returns
🔹 Revenue by Category
Highlights top revenue drivers
Enables category-level performance comparison
🔹 Marketplace Revenue Share
Visual breakdown of market contribution by category
Helps detect dominant vs niche segments
🔹 Orders Trend Over Time
Tracks monthly order growth
Reveals seasonality and demand spikes
🔹 Revenue Trend by Category
Shows sales variability and distribution
Identifies consistent vs volatile categories

📊 Key Insights
- Certain categories generate high revenue but also incur high freight costs, impacting profitability
- A few categories dominate overall sales, indicating dependency on limited segments
- Order volume shows strong growth followed by fluctuations, suggesting seasonality
- Some categories exhibit high variability, indicating inconsistent demand

🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Tableau – Data visualization & dashboarding

📈 Business Impact

This dashboard helps stakeholders:
- Optimize pricing and logistics strategies
- Focus on high-performing product categories
- Improve inventory and demand planning
- Make data-driven business decisions
